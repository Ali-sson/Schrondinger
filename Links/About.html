Our Story

Schrodinger Technologies Ltd was born out of a passion to solve one of humanity’s most pressing challenges: access to clean water. Founded in Nigeria in 2022, our journey began with a simple yet ambitious goal–to bring safe, affordable water solutions to underserved communities across West Africa. What started as an idea in a small workshop has grown into a groundbreaking venture. Armed with innovation, determination, and a deep commitment to making a difference, we developed our flagship product—the RienLight Water Box. This solar-powered water purification system transforms highly saline and polluted water into safe drinking water, all while being off-grid, affordable, and easy to maintain.

From disaster zones to rural villages, our systems have reached thousands of people, changing lives and empowering communities. As of 2024, we’ve impacted over 42,000 lives and are just getting started. Our work combines sustainability, innovation, and community partnership to create a ripple effect of positive change.

Every step of our journey is fueled by our belief that everyone deserves access to clean water. Join us as we continue to innovate, explore, and build a future where no one suffers from water scarcity. Together, we can quench the world’s thirst.
