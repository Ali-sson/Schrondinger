Our Approaches

At Schrodinger Technologies Ltd, we are committed to engineering excellence in water and renewable energy solutions. We design, develop, and implement cutting-edge water purification and wastewater treatment systems powered by sustainable energy sources. Our solutions serve households, communities, NGOs, refugee camps, schools, hospitals, processing companies, and agricultural irrigation projects.

Our Services

Hydro-Geological Survey

We conduct professional hydro-geological surveys to assess underground water availability, ensuring sustainable and efficient borehole drilling. Our expert analysis helps determine the best locations for water extraction.

Borehole Drilling

We provide reliable borehole drilling services for households, communities, and industries. Our modern equipment and skilled team ensure high-yield, long-lasting water solutions tailored to your needs.

Building and Industrial Plumbing

From residential homes to large-scale industries, we offer top-quality plumbing solutions that guarantee efficient water distribution and waste management systems.

Community Water Stations

We design and install solar-powered community water stations, providing safe and affordable drinking water to underserved areas. Our stations promote public health and environmental sustainability.

Water and Wastewater Treatment

We specialize in water and wastewater treatment solutions, designing and constructing systems that meet industrial, municipal, and domestic needs while ensuring environmental compliance.

Solar Installation for Water Facilities

We integrate renewable energy with water solutions by installing solar-powered systems for boreholes, treatment plants, and community water stations, reducing operational costs and ensuring uninterrupted water supply.
